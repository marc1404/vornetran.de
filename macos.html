macOS

Some applications that I like to use on macOS:

  • 1Password
    “Protect passwords, financial data, and more with the best password manager for macOS.”

  • AltTab
    “Brings the power of Windows alt-tab to macOS.”

  • Amphetamine
    “Keeps your Mac, and optionally its display(s), awake through a super simple on/off switch, or automatically through easy-to-configure triggers.”

  • Bruno
    “A Git-integrated, fully offline, and open-source API client.”

  • ChatGPT
    “Chat about email, screenshots, files, and anything on your screen.”

  • Docker
    “Enhances your development experience by offering a powerful, user-friendly platform for container management.”

  • Ghostty
    “A fast, feature-rich, and cross-platform terminal emulator that uses platform-native UI and GPU acceleration.”

  • Grammarly
    “Industry-leading writing assistance wherever you work, from apps to word processors to websites.”

  • Homebrew
    “The missing package manager for macOS.”

  • JetBrains Toolbox
    “Manage your IDEs the easy way.”

  • Maccy
    “Clipboard manager for macOS which does one job – keep your copy history at hand.”

  • Numi
    “Beautiful calculator.”

  • Obsidian
    “The free and flexible app for your private thoughts.”

  • Rectangle
    “Move and resize windows in macOS using keyboard shortcuts or snap areas.”

  • RunCat
    “Cat living in the menubar that tells you the CPU usage by its running speed.”

  • Transmission
    “A fast, easy, and free BitTorrent client.”

  • Velja
    “Powerful browser picker.”

  • VLC
    “Free and open source cross-platform multimedia player and framework that plays most multimedia files as well as DVDs, Audio CDs, VCDs, and various streaming protocols.”
